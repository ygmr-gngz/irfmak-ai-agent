Video Production Brief for İrfmak Makina Objective: Create a premium promotional video for İrfmak Makina focusing on the importance of choosing the right sewing machine, featuring Singer and Pfaff brands. The video should establish trust and authority while showcasing the brand's expertise in machine selection and support services. Video Structure: First Second: Begin with a close-up shot of a sewing machine or fabric, with immediate voiceover starting. Use dynamic motion to engage the audience right away. Visual Elements: - Display detailed shots of Pfaff and Singer sewing machines, highlighting their premium features. - Showcase stitching processes, needle movements, and fabric textures in close-up to emphasize quality and precision. - Include scenes from a workshop or textile production environment to convey professionalism and expertise. Voiceover: - Use the suggested Turkish voiceover: 'Pfaff dikiş makineleri, hassasiyet ve güçlü dikiş isteyenler için öne çıkar. Doğru makine seçimi, işinizin kalitesini doğrudan etkiler. İrfmak Makina, Singer ve Pfaff modellerinde doğru seçimi yapmanız için yanınızda. Satıştan servise kadar güvenilir destek sunar.' Subtitles: - Include Turkish subtitles that match the voiceover exactly, using clean, modern, and centered text. Ensure that they are visible throughout the video with subtle fade or scale animations. Highlight key terms like 'Hassasiyet' and 'Doğru Seçim' with slightly larger text. Sales Feeling: - The video must convey a premium commercial vibe. Build trust and authority around İrfmak Makina. Emphasize the direct impact of machine choice on quality without resorting to exaggerated sales tactics. Ending: - Conclude with a soft yet clear call to action: “Doğru makine için İrfmak Makina.” - Follow this with a very short brand outro, lasting 1.5 seconds, featuring: - 'İrfmak Makina' - 'Singer & Pfaff' - 'irfmak.com' - Ensure the outro is concise, avoiding long empty screens. On-Screen Text Rules: - Use very short text overlays (1-3 words) such as 'Pfaff', 'Hassasiyet', 'Güçlü Dikiş', 'Doğru Seçim', 'İrfmak Makina'. - Keep text large, clean, and within the safe area, ensuring no letters are cropped. - Apply a subtle zoom-in/scale-up animation effect for text appearances, ensuring a modern, premium feel. Brand Tone: - Maintain a tone that is premium, trustworthy, clean, and professional throughout the video. This brief outlines a clear direction for creating a compelling promotional video that effectively communicates İrfmak Makina's brand values and expertise in the sewing machine market.
